## Why This Caught My Eye

Scientists mapped the brain of a fruit fly and built an emulator from it. That emulated brain behaves like a fruit fly.

The distinction matters: this isn't a trained simulation that approximates brain behavior. It's an emulated copy. That's a different and much weirder category of thing entirely
