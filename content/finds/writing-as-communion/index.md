## Why This Caught My Eye

Good writing finds its shape by finding an audience and letting them tell you what matters. The feedback loop between writer and reader isn't marketing, it's craft. The audience doesn't just receive the work; they complete it.

This hit differently as I'm building out my blog and newsletter. I've been thinking about the audience as a distribution problem. Karlsson reframes it as a writing problem.
