## Why This Caught My Eye

This article introduces the concept of a _milieu_: the particular set of influences that surround us. It goes on to state that our milieu not only defines our worldview but also, from a systems perspective, is the input to our output.

A lot to unpack here, but like a growth mindset, it reframes what we thought was fixed. We can take control of our milieu to change our world.
